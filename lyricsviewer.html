📱 About This Activity

This is a simple Android Studio lyrics viewer app for the OPM artist Sugarcane. The UI demonstrates two Activities side by side — the home song list on the left and the lyrics detail on the right.

  • Home Activity — Displays the song list with album art covers for all 6 tracks: Bituin, Dalangin, Gabi, Kung Maging Akin Ka, Leonora, and Maria Clara.
  • Lyrics Detail Activity — Full song lyrics on a deep starry-night background, centered in white text. Each screen matches the aesthetic from the original app screenshots.
  • Interactive — Use the tabs beneath the right phone to switch between any song's lyrics view instantly.
  • Navigation — A translucent back button sits at the top-left, with a persistent bottom navigation bar (Songs / Favorites / Settings).
  • Design — Dark cosmic background with white serif typography mirrors the intimate, poetic feel of Sugarcane's songs.
